5. Rule-Based Optimization Engine

Campaign health rules:
- If pacing < 90% and CPA is below benchmark, classify as “Underpacing but efficient”
- If pacing < 90% and CPA is above benchmark, classify as “Underpacing and inefficient”
- If pacing is between 90% and 110% and CPA is below benchmark, classify as “Healthy”
- If pacing > 110% and CPA is above benchmark, classify as “Overspending and inefficient”

Site exclusion rules:
Recommend site exclusion only if enough data exists:
- Spend > user-defined threshold
- AND conversions = 0 OR CPA > 2x benchmark
- AND viewability is below goal OR out-of-geo is above threshold OR IVT is above threshold

Zip optimization rules:
Recommend zip removal/reduction if:
- Spend is meaningful
- AND conversions are zero or CPA is poor
- AND out-of-geo is high
- AND impressions/clicks are enough

Recommend zip expansion if:
- Campaign is underpacing
- AND CPA is below benchmark
- AND viewability is healthy
- AND out-of-geo is under control
- AND approved zip list has additional zips

PMP deal rules:
Flag PMP deal issues if:
- Floor CPM is higher than bid or target CPM
- Win rate is low
- Spend is very low
- CPA is poor
- Viewability is poor
- Deal has good KPI but low scale
